Hometown

Banyuwangi is a city located at the eastern tip of Java Island, known as the "Sunrise of Java". The city is famous for its extraordinary natural beauty, from mountains, beaches, to national parks. The unique Osing culture and various international festivals make Banyuwangi widely recognized. Surrounded by mountains and the sea, Banyuwangi offers a serene atmosphere and the hospitality of local people. The city is also the gateway to Bali and a favorite tourist destination in Indonesia.
