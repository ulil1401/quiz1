Tourist Places

  • Ijen Crater: Known for its spectacular blue fire phenomenon, Ijen Crater offers a unique hiking experience.
  • Baluran National Park: Called the "Africa of Java," this national park is famous for its vast savannahs and diverse wildlife such as banteng and peacocks.
  • De Djawatan: This forest with giant rain trees provides a magical atmosphere similar to Fangorn Forest in the Lord of the Rings films.
  • Bangsring Underwater: Suitable for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts, this place is a coral reef conservation area with various ornamental fish.
  • Green Bay: The bay with beautiful turquoise water and white sand, is located within the Meru Betiri National Park area.
  • Sukamade Beach: Part of Meru Betiri National Park, this beach is a turtle conservation area where turtles lay their eggs.
  • Tabuhan Island: This uninhabited island offers activities such as snorkeling, walking, and taking photos in the middle of the Bali Strait.

FYI De Djawatan is the closest destination to my house. Only 1 km from my house
